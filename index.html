About

I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the Indian Institutes of Technology Kanpur. I am working on Satellite Internet, Space Networking with Dr. Amitangshu Pal and Dr. Debopam Bhattacherjee.

My research interests lie in the implementation and simulation of Networked Systems and the Internet of Things.

Before joining IIT-K in 2022, I obtained an M.Tech. Degree in Information Technology from the University of Hyderabad, collaborated with Institute for Development and Research in Banking Technology (IDRBT). Dr. Satish Narayana Srirama was my thesis advisor. Earlier I obtained a B.Tech. Degree in Computer Science & Engineering from the Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, formally known as the West Bengal University of Technology.
